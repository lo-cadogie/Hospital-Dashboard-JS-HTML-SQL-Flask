Hospital Boarding

Use the interactive charts below to explore the dataset in regards to hospital boarding in the Emergency Department. Boarding is defined as the number of minutes that a patients is waiting in the Emergency Department, after the physician has determined their course of treatment. Typically, these are patients that are waiting to be admitted into the hospital or waiting on a transfer to another hospital (for a higher level of care). Unforuntately, with the tridemic, the boarding in the hospital is getting out of control.
